Double

A minimalist android game in which you need to avoid enemies in both screens at once.
There are a lot of different and unique achievements which allow you to unlock new skins.

The game is no longer avaible on the Google Play store, but you can download the APK here.


Music: Pierfrancesco Palma

Rotate

Rotate is a puzzle game about perspective. Your goal is to get the ball to the end of the level, but you are not restricted to a single plane and you explore the level from different angles.

The game can be found here.

You can also try a tech demo of a 3d version of this game that I am currently working on here.

Round Pong

A new take on the classic game Pong, in which the action takes place in a circle.
It's a small game done in a few days to practice experimenting with multiplayer, even so can be really funny to play with a friend.

If you want to try it out you can download it here.

About me

Hi! I am a years old guy from Italy.

Since I was a child I have always loved to create and learn new tools to realize my ideas.
I remember that when I was very young I used to make things using anything I could find in my house. Then at the age of 14 I approached videogames programming and I stated discovering the endless possibilities that the digital medium could offer. This amazed me so much that brought me to learn more and more about digital art in general.
I really believe that videogames are the perfect manifestation of the freedom and creativity I always seek, and I would love to explore this world as much as I can in my life.
